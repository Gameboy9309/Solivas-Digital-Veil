This is the first post in my digital garden. Unlike a traditional blog, this site is meant to grow — not just accumulate chronological posts.

## What a digital garden is

A digital garden is a personal website that's less like a magazine and more like a notebook. Things here get updated as my thinking evolves. A note might start as a rough seed of an idea and slowly grow into something more refined. Nothing is ever truly "finished."

The format frees me from the pressure of publishing polished takes. I can think in public, make mistakes, and revise.

## What I plan to write about

Honestly, whatever I find interesting. Probably a mix of:

- Things I'm learning
- Projects I'm working on
- Observations about the world
- Ideas I keep returning to

If you're reading this, welcome. Pull up a chair and stay a while.
