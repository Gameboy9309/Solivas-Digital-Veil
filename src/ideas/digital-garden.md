title: What makes a good digital garden
date: 2026-04-19

---

I've been thinking about what actually makes a digital garden *feel* like a garden versus just a blog with a fancy name.

A few properties I think matter:

- **Non-linear** — you can enter from any node, not just the front page
- **Bidirectional** — ideas link to and from each other
- **Dated loosely** — the "freshness" of an idea matters less than its depth
- **Visible growth** — readers should be able to see when something was last tended

The hardest part is letting go of the need to publish finished things.
